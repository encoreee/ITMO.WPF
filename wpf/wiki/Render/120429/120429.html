DataGrid Tips & Tricks: Custom Sort

If you find that the performance of DataGrid's built-in sorting is less than optimal in your application, we recommend that you implement a custom sorting algorithm. You can find instructions for implementing custom sort on the WPF Performance Blog. WPF performance expert Jossef Goldberg has written a two-part series on Improving WPF DataGrid Sorting Performance (Part 2 is available here) (the smaple that uses the released version of the Toolkit is here ).
